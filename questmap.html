Questmap Overview

The Questmap is a quest system where you need to defeat monsters on a specific map. It is a combination of multiple monsters, making it a challenging and exciting experience.

Each quest offers a valuable reward that makes it worth completing. However, you can only complete this quest once during your gameplay.
